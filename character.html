Mr. Pop & Sip

Overview

Meet Mr. Pop and Sip, a delightful popcorn and drink character that embodies fun and relaxation. Mr. Pop and Sip is perfect for engaging audiences and adding a whimsical touch to any project. This cheerful duo captures the essence of enjoyment, making them ideal for packaging design or promotional materials.

Personality

  • Energetic
  • Fun

Tools

Typeface

Project

  • Individual Project
  • Module: Fundamentals of Design

Color Scheme

This 2 set of color schemes feature bright, vibrant hues that evoke a sense of liveliness and cheerfulness. These warm colors enhance the fun and playful nature of Mr. Pop and Sip, creating an inviting atmosphere that captivates and delights viewers.

Sketches

This sketch brings Mr. Pop and Sip to life, showcasing a playful popcorn character and a refreshing drink, both radiating fun and relaxation. With lively expressions and dynamic poses, this illustration captures the essence of good times and tasty treats.

Variations

I have created 2 set of variations, one is to create a different kind of expressions/faces to make the characters more lively and the another set is to create a realistic body movements.

Variations

By making use of different vairations in terms of expressions and movements, I created a total of 5 characters design with varying colours to give a sense of fun visual designs.

Final Designs

DESIGN NO.1

The first design features a vibrant yellow tote bag, designed for those who love bold colors and want to stand out. Its lively hue exudes energy and positivity, making it a perfect accessory for brightening up any outfit.

DESIGN NO.2

The second design consists of a soft light blue background, appealing to those who prefer a more serene and subtle aesthetic. Its calming color creates a sense of tranquility, making it an ideal choice for everyday use.
