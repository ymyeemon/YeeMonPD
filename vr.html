The Last Shot

Overview

The Last Shot is a Virtual Reality Escape Room Game, catering the immersive murder/thriller environment with 4 different puzzles to the players with well-defined graphics and thrilling audios. This game was design and developed by using Unity Software along with the C# programming language.

Moodboard

My moodboard for this game captures a tense atmosphere with subdued lighting and classic, refined furnishings. Inspired by the sleek yet ominous aesthetic of John Wick, the setting combines elegance with a hint of danger, perfect for immersing players in the suspenseful world of a hitman on a mission.

so, what's the narrative?

The player takes on the role of a hitman sent to eliminate a high-profile target, alongside a small team. But when they reach the target's house, a brutal fight breaks out with the target's allies. The player’s memory fades at this point, and the player eventually wake up locked in a room, surrounded by evidence of their teammates’ murders. Now, the player must uncover clues and solve puzzles in order to escape the room and complete their mission.

3D Models

I've also created a custom 3D piano model in Unity to add realism to the VR game. Starting with basic shapes, I designed the body, keys, and pedals, then added textures and lighting to give it a classic, slightly worn look. This piano serves as both a visual asset and a crucial part of the puzzle-solving experience.

Final Outcome

1st Puzzle

In the first room, the player solves a puzzle by finding clues to play the notes "E, B, C" on the piano. A letter on the table and a book with note placements guide the player. When played in the correct order, each key stays down, and a hidden box with the door key appears behind the piano, allowing the player to move to the hallway for 2nd puzzle.

2nd Puzzle

In the hallway, the player finds a wooden board with arrows indicating the correct positions for four levers. They can move all the levers initially, but once the first lever is set correctly, it becomes unmovable. The player must then adjust the remaining levers in order. Once all four are correctly positioned, the door to the next room will automatically open.

3rd Puzzle

In the second room, the player finds a keypad and a clue on the desk with four numbers near letters. By spelling the word "LAST," the player determines the correct number sequence: "1680." Entering the numbers reveals a hidden key box beside the keypad. The player then takes the key and returns to the first room for the final puzzle.

4th Puzzle

Using the key from the last puzzle, the player opens a drawer in the first room to find four triangular stones of different colors. To escape, they must place the blue triangular stone into the socket near the door. The socket only accepts the correct stone, and once it is placed, the final escape door will automatically open.
