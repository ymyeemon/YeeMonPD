Hobby Hues

Overview

This stickers set represent my hobbies or things I love to do in my free time as part of my personal branding. Each one reflects a unique aspect of my interests, combining vibrant colors and playful design to add a personal touch to my work.

Personality

  • Lively
  • Cheerful
  • Fun

Tools

  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Photoshop

Typeface

  • Arial Rounded MT Bold

Project

  • Individual Project
  • Module: Fundamentals of Design
  • Year 2, Semester 1, 2024

Color Scheme

The sticker set features a vibrant color scheme of red, yellow, orange, and various shades of blue, chosen to convey warmth, energy, and a sense of playfulness.

Sketches

I started out with a detailed sketches to create stickers design that represent each of my hobbies such as photogrphy, watching films, listening to music, reading and travelling to have a more accurate representation of my personality.

Final Designs

Design 01.

This design represents my hobby of enjoying music and playing musical instruments in my free time. Every small decorations you can see in the background such as guitar, disco ball and music notes were drawn in Adobe Illustrator as well.

Design 02.

Sticker Number 2 represents my interests in watching films/movies and I have created popcorn, drink, movie tickets and camera to be put in the background to make the sticker more stand out.

Design 03.

This sticker is a quite simple yet bright and inviting design to shows my love for reading. Featuring a cozy design with a book as the focal point, it reflects my passion for getting lost in multiple stories.

Design 04.

This vibrant sticker illustrates my passion for photography. Through this sticker, I hope you can see my love for capturing moments and perspectives through the lens, anywhere, anytime.

Design 05.

This is a lively sticker design that reflects my love for travelling. It embodies my enthusiasm for exploring new places and cultures, all brought together with my colorful and cheerful design.
