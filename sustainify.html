Sustainify

Overview

This logo for the sustainable mobile application called Sustainify, embodies the spirit of eco-friendliness and positivity. Designed to resonate with users, the logo aims to reflects and inspire individuals to embrace eco-conscious habits in a fun and engaging way.

Personality

Tools

Project

Color Scheme

The main colors used for this logo design were mainly derived from the colors we see in nature such as light green, blue and yellow to give a sense of earthy and natural feelings through the visuals.

Sketches

I started out with a sketch that could depicts a happy and relaxed Earth walking, symbolizing an eco-friendly lifestyle. The playful concept aims to inspire users to embrace sustainability with joy and ease.

Variations

I’ve created 3 different variations of the logo for the sustainable mobile application, each designed to convey the spirit of eco-friendliness and user engagement. These variations explore different styles and elements while maintaining a cohesive theme.
